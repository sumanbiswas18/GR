History

2005 TO 2018

The Green Foundation was founded in August 2005 and is a non-profit organization that aims to assist and uplift rural communities with the support of environmental projects.

We have raised more than R 4 million to date and have won the Hope Award in 2007 and 2010. To date, we have assisted 45 rural communities in South Africa, Botswana and Zambia and planted more than 30,000 trees. For a full outline of our projects per area, please see our project booklet, attached.
